## Study Notes

### 1. Welcome! Today is single vowel speaking practice!

Hello everyone! Today is Korean speaking practice time! We'll practice pronouncing the single vowels we learned, and also try out simple greetings.

### 2. Pronounce 'ㅏ' (A)! Open your mouth wide!

Alright, the first single vowel is 'ㅏ' (A). Look in the mirror, open your mouth wide, and say 'Ah!' Your mouth shape is important.

### 3. Pronounce 'ㅓ' (Eo)! Drop your jaw slightly!

Next is 'ㅓ' (Eo). Open your mouth slightly less wide than for 'A', drop your jaw slightly, and say 'Eo!' The pronunciation changes, right?

### 4. Pronounce 'ㅗ' (O)! Round your lips!

This time it's 'ㅗ' (O). Round your lips slightly forward and say 'Oh!' You need to move your lips well.

### 5. Pronounce 'ㅜ' (U)! Protrude your lips!

Next is 'ㅜ' (U). Round your lips even more forward than for 'O' and say 'Oo!' Your lips should protrude more.

### 6. Pronounce 'ㅡ' (Eu)! Pull your lips sideways!

This time it's 'ㅡ' (Eu). Pull your lips slightly to the sides without opening your mouth wide and say 'Eu!' Don't move your lips too much.

### 7. Pronounce 'ㅣ' (I)! Smile broadly!

Alright, it's 'ㅣ' (I). Pull the corners of your mouth wide to the sides, as if you're smiling broadly, and say 'Ee!' This might be the easiest one.

### 8. Pronounce 'ㅐ' (Ae)! Open your mouth wider than 'I'!

Now for 'ㅐ' (Ae). Open your mouth slightly wider than for 'Ee' and say 'Ae!' It's similar to 'E' but your mouth is more open.

### 9. Pronounce 'ㅔ' (E)! Open your mouth less than 'Ae'!

The last single vowel is 'ㅔ' (E). Open your mouth slightly less wide than for 'Ae' and naturally say 'Eh!' You'll get used to it quickly with practice.

### 10. Role-play: Hello! I am [Name]!

Shall we practice greetings using the single vowels now? Try saying 'Annyeong! Naneun [Your Name] imnida.' (Hello! I am [Your Name].) With confidence!

### 11. Class dismissed! See you next time!

That's all for today's Korean speaking class. How was practicing single vowel pronunciation and greetings? Consistent practice is the most important! See you next time!
